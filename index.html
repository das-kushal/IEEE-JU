About the Event



The Machine Learning Accelerator Summit is a tiny step taken by the IEEE Student Branch, Jadavpur University to help students, engineers and tech enthusiasts live the AI dream.
We look forward to decode the most sought-after concept in today's world, down to the very fundamentals. It will be an introduction to Machine Learning and AI with hands-on coding, guided by the mentors, followed by a Hackathon.
